Redesign or brand new? Redesign of current eBay page
Type of Site Shopping/ Selling
Goals My client hopes to sell items, purchase new items to sell, gain new customers, retain current custoers and gain info from customers
AUDIENCES ISSUES CONCERNS NEEDS APPROACH

Collectors

61% of Americans would describe themselves as collectors. Coins are the most collected out of the general category followed by toys and then trading cards, art is 9th on the list and comics are 12th.The average comic collector is a Millennial and the average coin collector is a Boomer. Men are more likely to collect than women (70% vs 51%). The most used platform by collectors is eBay. 		ISSUES
  • Many collecting niches
  • Most collectors tend to over value their collection
NEEDS
  • Verification of purchased item's authenticity
  • Secure transactions
CONCERNS
  • Store (seller) reliability(good customer service, items being sold are authentic)
 Niches (issue)
  • Having a broad array of items which can appeal to many niches
  • Having days dedicated to showcasing specific niches could help with drawing in different crowds (they know which days to look out for the items they are looking for)
  • Clear and accurate inventory categories will make browsing niches easier

Deal Enthusiasts/Coupon Clipper

The average deal enthusiast / "coupon clipper" is a female boomer/ millennial, the income demographic can range from working class to middle/upper middle class. The most used shopping platforms used are Amazon, Walmart and Target. Most have a medium level of computer literacy.		 ISSUES
  • Expects to save money
NEEDS
  • organizable/ sortable categories
CONCERNS
  • Seller reliability
 Saving Money (issue)
    Having deals on certain days or a customer loyalty program (make 5 purchases and get a discount on the 6th)
Organization (need)
    This need can be easily met by having well thought out categories of inventory that an be easily built upon and expanded.

Electronics Collector/Repairer

The average electronics repairer is a male millennial with a working class income and a medium to high computer literacy. The most popular sites used are eBay, Newegg and Amazon. 		Issues
  • Odd/unusual product size (so shipping items to them may be somewhat expensive)
Needs
  • Customer testimonials are more important for this demographic since ensuring the antique/art being shipped remains intact during the shipping process is important.
  • Reliable shipping (not necessarily fast)
  • Numerous pictures of items
  • Authentication of items sold (are they actually what they say they are)
 Unusual Shipping (issue)
  • Customer testimonials and increased customer engagement may help with the uncertainty around shipping, as long as client can actually maintain a good reputation around shipping than good ratings would ease new client's anxieties. Maybe a "Customer of the week" blog post of customers posting a picture of their recently purchased item and their entire experience.
